## Idea
KisanSeeva is a digital marketplace that connects rural farmers directly with agricultural vehicle service providers(owners and drivers), eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair, transparent pricing through a community‑regulated annual survey system. It solves the real‑world problem of price exploitation and lack of access to timely farm machinery by letting farmers book services, track drivers live via GPS, and pay digitally – all in a simple mobile‑friendly interface. The platform empowers village communities, reduces farming costs, and accelerates Digital India by bringing smart, trust‑based farm mechanization to the grassroots.

-> KisanSeva is a digital marketplace that connects smallholder farmers with verified agricultural machinery owners, solving the crisis of unreliable, overpriced tractor and equipment hire in rural India.

-> Its core innovation is a community‑regulated pricing engine: every June, villagers submit service‑cost suggestions through a transparent survey, and the admin publishes locally approved price bands that all driver quotations must honour, eliminating arbitrary rates.

-> Farmers create geotagged service requests; nearby drivers receive instant real‑time notifications, submit compliant quotes, and once accepted, share live GPS tracking so farmers can monitor arrival and progress.

-> The platform integrates digital payments (Razorpay), automatic GST‑compliant invoicing, and a rating system, creating a formal, cashless, and trustworthy service cycle.

-> By replacing opaque bargaining with participatory, data‑driven pricing and offering end‑to‑end visibility, KisanSeva prevents exploitation, raises driver incomes, and drives digital empowerment in the last‑mile agricultural economy.
